## Overview

Sistem Pertanian Berbasis IoT is an award-winning project built for **Impact National Hackathon 2024**, where the team competed against more than 200 participants. The solution focused on agriculture modernization through automatic pest detection and smart irrigation.

## Key Contributions

- Developed an IoT-based solution for pest detection and irrigation automation
- Integrated microcontroller systems with real-time data storage
- Contributed to the user-facing interface for presenting system output clearly
- Participated in field research activities in **Desa Wisata Leuwimalang**
- Presented the solution and its potential impact to industry practitioners

## Why It Matters

The project was designed to support:

- more responsive agricultural monitoring
- scalable data collection in the field
- practical automation for modern farming workflows

## Recognition

- **1st Place Winner** at Impact National Hackathon 2024
- Demonstrated the ability to combine hardware, software, and field research into a deployable concept
